Intro

Tru-Skills Hub cc, a recruitment and labour broking services based in Johannesburg, renders recruitment consultancy to suit your needs. It emerges from a previously disadvantaged background and is a B-BBEE level 3, 100% Black Owned, controlled and managed.

The business is divided into two divisions the Recruitment and Labour Broking services. Tru-Skill Hub cc believes that the biggest contributing factor to any entity’s profitability is its people and that an educated and skilled workforce is the one major differentiating factor between companies in the same industry. To this end it aims to provide the best personnel to its clients.

Tru-Skills Hub cc source & provide personnel that match the specific requirements of its client and do so within the shortest possible time to avoid and prevent disruption in the work place. Personnel services that add value, loyalty to its employers and create a level of stability in the work place. By the way, check out some of our awesome work.

Services

Personnel Placement

We offer personnel placement services with integrated customer relationship management solutions that include the following:
  • Placement: Filling skills gaps through placement of screened candidates;
  • Consulting: Identifying skills needs for companies operating in selected target markets;

Call Centre Recruitment

We also provide highly qualified and well-trained call centre staff from management to agent to assist clients in adding value to their customer interface processes. We handle the full recruitment function, encompassing permanent, temporary and contract employment.

Flexible Staffing

We assist our clients in sourcing candidates who wish to work on a temporary and contract basis. This service assists the client in making use of candidates for a shorter period. Our service in this regard consists of obtaining the required candidate, processing the payroll administration and attending to the necessary statutory requirement.
